Enter a league's ID number to view stats relating to the top 50 players in the league including:

  • Who has the most goals, assists, clean sheets, bonus points etc.
  • Who has the highest scoring defense, midfield etc.
  • Who is the best at picking their captain
  • Who is each team's MVP
  • Who leaves the most points on the bench
  • The favourite formation of each member


To find out a league's ID, click on the league and see the ID in the URL


Tips

  1. Click the banner at the top of the screen to return to the home screen
  2. Every column in the main tables can be sorted to quickly find the highest/lowest values
  3. Click on the Manager or League name in the top left corner of each view to go to that manager's team or that league's page on the main FPL website
  4. When looking at a League, you can click on a Manager's name in any of the the main tables to go to the Manager View for that person
  5. Bookmark the search result so you don't need to re-enter your Manager or League ID in future. You can also share the link with your friends so that they can bypass the search and be brought straight to the results page
